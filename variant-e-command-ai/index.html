FAQ

Honest answers.

Will it slow down my Mac?

The capture daemon polls every 3–7 minutes and runs Haiku-class inference on the OCR window. On an M1 it adds about 2–4% sustained CPU and ~120MB RAM — less than a single Chrome tab playing video. There's a hard daily token cap so cost-and-CPU spikes are mathematically bounded. If you ever feel it, hit ambient pause today in your terminal.

What if I uninstall — what happens to my data?

The installer's uninstall step revokes the device on our end and deletes the local SQLite + macOS Keychain entry. Your cloud data stays read-only for 30 days as a grace window (in case you reinstall), then it's purged for good. If you want it gone now, hit "Delete account" in settings and it's destroyed within 24 hours.

Does it work with Apple Mail, Outlook, Spark, or Superhuman?

Yes. The capture is OCR-based on the active window, not Gmail-API-based — so it watches Apple Mail, Outlook, Spark, Superhuman, and any other mail client that puts a "From: …" header on screen. Gmail in Chrome/Safari is the most-tested path, but the others work without configuration.

What model powers the AI?

A mix. Local Haiku-class models run on your Mac for screen analysis and entity extraction (privacy). Frontier models (Claude Sonnet / GPT-4-class) run in the cloud only for content you've explicitly shared — drafts, summaries, queries. We swap models as better ones ship; you don't have to think about it.

Will the AI hallucinate or make up facts about my deals?

The AI is grounded — it can only answer from records you have. Every factual claim it makes about a contact must cite at least one source signal (an actual email, Slack thread, or transcript). If it can't cite, it has to say "I don't have evidence for that." Drafts and summaries link back to the underlying activity so you can audit before sending.

Does it watch literally everything on my screen?

No. It watches the apps and domains on your allowlist (Gmail, Slack, LinkedIn, Calendly, Zoom, Outlook by default). Banking, password managers, crypto wallets, and incognito windows are denied at the bundle-ID level and cannot be enabled. Every other app is default-deny — you opt in per app, and you can mute anything any time.

Can I import contacts from HubSpot, Pipedrive, or Attio?

Yes. CSV import works out of the box. Direct importers for HubSpot, Pipedrive, Attio, and Folk pull contacts, companies, deals, and history. The AI auto-enriches everything on import. Most users finish in under 10 minutes.

Can I use the AI features without sharing my screen?

Yes — the AI features (scoring, drafts, queries, summaries, enrichment, stall detection) all work on data you import or sync from email/calendar without the desktop daemon. The auto-logging is what requires the daemon. You can run with or without it.
