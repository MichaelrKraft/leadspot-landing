FAQ

Honest answers.

Is the AI just a chat panel bolted onto a regular CRM?

No — that's the whole pitch. Most "AI CRMs" are HubSpot with a chat sidebar. Ghostlog is built around the AI: auto-logging is AI, scoring is AI-grounded, drafts are AI, summaries are AI, queries are AI, enrichment is AI, stall detection is AI. The CRM is the substrate; the AI is the product.

What model powers the AI features?

A mix. Local Haiku-class models run on your Mac for screen analysis and entity extraction (privacy-preserving). Frontier models (Claude Sonnet / GPT-4-class) run in the cloud only for content you've explicitly shared — drafts, summaries, queries. We swap models as better ones ship; you don't have to think about it.

Does the AI watch literally everything on my screen?

No. It watches the apps and domains on your allowlist (Gmail, LinkedIn, Slack, Calendly, Zoom by default). Banking, password managers, and health portals are muted at the OS level and can't be enabled. You can mute any app any time. The audit log is local — you can grep what was sent to the cloud and delete entries.

Can I import contacts from HubSpot, Pipedrive, or Attio?

Yes. CSV import works out of the box. Direct importers for HubSpot, Pipedrive, Attio, and Folk pull contacts, companies, deals, and historical activity. The AI auto-enriches everything on import so you're not staring at empty fields on day one. Most users finish migration in under 10 minutes.

How does this compare to Gong or Clari?

Gong and Clari are sales-team tools priced for revenue orgs of 20–50+ reps ($1.6k/seat/yr plus five-figure platform fees, six-week deployment). They watch calls. Ghostlog is built for the founder doing the selling, watches the entire desktop, costs $39/mo, and installs in 4 minutes. Different tools for different stages of company.

Will it work without internet?

Capture and entity matching keep running offline. Cloud features (AI drafts, queries that hit the embedding index, enrichment lookups) need an internet connection. When you reconnect, your local activity log syncs.

What if I cancel?

Your CRM data is yours. Export everything as CSV. The daemon stops running. We delete your account and the per-user encryption key — observations become unrecoverable from our side within 30 days.
