FAQ

Honest answers.

Does Ghostlog watch literally everything on my screen?

No. It watches the apps and domains on your allowlist (Gmail, LinkedIn, Slack, Calendly, Zoom by default). Banking, password managers, and health portals are muted permanently and can't be enabled. You can mute any app at any time.

Where do screenshots and OCR text go?

To your local machine, encrypted with a key in your macOS Keychain. They never leave. The cloud only ever receives a redacted summary (e.g. "exchanged email with jane@acme.com at 11:42") — never the actual content.

Can I see what was sent to the cloud?

Yes. The local audit log shows every observation Ghostlog promoted to the cloud, with the original local source. You can grep it, export it, or delete entries.

Does this work without LeadSpot?

Right now, no — Ghostlog ships with LeadSpot included. We may add HubSpot, Attio, and Folk in the future depending on what design partners ask for.

Will it sync between my Mac and my phone?

Activity data syncs to LeadSpot's web app, so you can read it from any device. The capture daemon runs only on Mac for now (Windows is on the roadmap).

What does the AI actually do beyond auto-logging activity?

A lot — and it's why the CRM doesn't feel like a database. The AI scores leads from real signals (response cadence, deal velocity, opens) instead of demographics. It drafts follow-up emails grounded in your past conversations. It detects deals about to stall before they actually stall. It answers natural-language questions ("which leads opened my email twice but haven't replied?") with actual records, not charts. It auto-enriches new contacts from email signature, company, role, and LinkedIn. Every contact has a rolling 3-line AI summary so you know where things stand without re-reading 40 emails.

How does this compare to HubSpot, Pipedrive, or Gong?

HubSpot and Pipedrive are databases you have to fill in. Ghostlog is a CRM that fills itself in and tells you what to do next. Gong watches calls only and is priced for sales teams of 50+ ($1.6k/seat/yr plus a five-figure platform fee). Ghostlog watches the entire desktop, costs $39/mo, and is built for the founder doing the selling. If you have a sales team, an admin, and a CRM ops person, use Salesforce + Gong. If you're the founder doing this between every other thing on your plate, Ghostlog is built for you.

Can I import contacts from my existing CRM?

Yes. CSV import works out of the box. We have direct importers for HubSpot, Pipedrive, Attio, and Folk that pull contacts, companies, deals, and historical activity. Most users finish migration in under 10 minutes. The AI auto-enriches everything on import so you're not staring at a wall of empty fields on day one.

What if I cancel?

Your CRM data is yours. You can export everything as CSV. The daemon stops running. We delete your account and the per-user encryption key — promoted observations become unrecoverable from our side within 30 days.
